Hotels Group Amelia Management System

Project Overview

A comprehensive Hotel Management System for the Amelia Group, designed to manage multiple hotels across different locations and types.

Key Features

  • Multi-hotel management system
  • Flexible room classification (type, view, floor, amenities)
  • Seasonal pricing management
  • Client registration and loyalty program
  • Reservation and stay tracking

Hotel Diversity

Hotels can be categorized into:

  • Mountain hotels
  • Beach hotels
  • City hotels
  • Optional amenities: swimming pool, sauna, golf, gym, private parking

Room Management

  • Room types: Single, Double, Triple
  • Views: Mountain, Sea, Indoor Garden
  • Maximum 10 rooms per floor
  • Unique room numbering system
  • Seasonal pricing variations

User Roles and Access

  • General Manager: Full system access
  • Hotel Manager: Access to managed hotels
  • Maintenance Manager: Maintenance incident tracking
  • Receptionist: Single hotel information access
  • Clients: Personal profile and booking access

Client Benefits

  • Group-wide client registration
  • Preferential status after 3 stays
  • 5% discount for preferential clients
  • Book rooms across all group hotels

Key System Rules

  • Automatic end date suggestion for reservations
  • Reservation date validations
  • Automatic age calculation
  • Flexible identification (passport/citizen card)
  • Bulk check-in/checkout management

Management Dashboard

  • Revenue comparison between hotels
  • Top 5 clients visualization
  • Charts and detailed reporting
